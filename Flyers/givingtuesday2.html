UGA Expansion

A group of former interns brought The Sandwich Project to Athens by launching a new University of Georgia chapter. Now, UGA students are hosting sandwich-making events, supporting peers facing food insecurity, and expanding TSP's reach into Athens-Clarke County.

Community Partnership

We partnered with Solidarity Sandy Springs and Giving Grace to support homebound families. They packed grocery boxes for families with limited mobility and children, while TSP added fresh sandwiches and handled delivery. Together, we ensured consistent community care.
